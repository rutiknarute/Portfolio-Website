Overview

In today’s fast-paced eCommerce industry, data analytics is crucial for making informed decisions. I took on the challenge of analyzing an Ecommerce Sales Platform using Power BI, with the goal of uncovering valuable insights and presenting them in an easy-to-understand, interactive dashboard. This project highlights my skills in data visualization, data analysis, and dashboard design, as well as my ability to transform raw data into actionable insights.

Project Objectives

  • Analyze sales trends, profitability, and customer behavior to identify key drivers of growth.
  • Explore product categories, states, and payment modes to optimize business strategies.
  • Build an interactive Power BI dashboard to enable real-time filtering and dynamic exploration of metrics.

Data Analysis and Key Insights

Sales Performance & Profitability

  • The total sales revenue for the period was ₹438,000, with a total profit of ₹37,000. Sales were highest in the fourth quarter, particularly in December, due to holiday shopping. However, there was a noticeable drop in sales during June and July, indicating a seasonal slowdown in the middle of the year.0

Customer Behavior & Product Categories

  • In terms of customer behavior and product categories, clothing made up the majority of items sold at 63%, followed by electronics at 21% and furniture at 17%. Among subcategories, printers and bookcases were the most profitable. Key customers like Harivansh, Madhav, and Madan Mohan contributed significantly to the overall revenue.

Regional Sales Distribution

  • Regionally, Maharashtra was the top-performing state, with Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh following closely behind. Analyzing sales by state helped identify potential areas for market growth.

Payment Mode Preferences

  • When it comes to payment preferences, Cash on Delivery (COD) was the most popular method, accounting for 44% of transactions, followed by UPI at 21% and debit/credit cards. This information is useful for shaping marketing strategies and improving payment options for customers.

Conclusion

This project not only strengthened my technical expertise in Power BI but also deepened my understanding of business analytics. By visualizing raw data into an actionable story, I demonstrated how businesses can uncover valuable insights and make impactful decisions.
