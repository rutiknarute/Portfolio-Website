Walmart Sales Analytics

Overview

This project involves analyzing transactional data from multiple Walmart branches to derive actionable business insights. By using SQL and Python, the data was queried, processed, and interpreted to support decisions in sales strategy, customer behavior analysis, and operational efficiency.

Project Objectives

  • Analyze sales patterns across Walmart branches based on time, location, and product category.
  • Identify trends in customer payment methods and purchasing behavior.
  • Measure product category performance by customer ratings and sales volume.
  • Determine the busiest periods and optimal staffing times for branches.
  • Explore city and branch-level insights to support location-specific strategies.

Data Analysis and Key Insights

Branch & Category Performance:

  • Identified the highest-rated product category per branch, highlighting top-performing departments for customer satisfaction.
  • Pinpointed busiest days for each branch using weekday breakdowns, aiding workforce planning and promotions.

Payment Methods & Sales Volume:

  • Determined most frequently used payment method by branch, valuable for payment system optimization.
  • Calculated total items sold per payment method, revealing trends in customer preference and transaction size.

Time & Demand Patterns:

  • Analyzed time-of-day activity, identifying the most active time slots (Morning, Afternoon, Evening) per branch for sales.
  • Offered insights into hourly traffic trends, helping Walmart align store resources and marketing offers.
